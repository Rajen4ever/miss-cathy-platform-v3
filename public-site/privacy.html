Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 18, 2026

Miss Cathy is a personal AI chief-of-staff product. This policy explains what information the app may process, how it is used, and the choices available to the user.

Information processed

  • Account information such as email address and profile display name
  • User-created content such as tasks, notes, projects, savepoints, content drafts, health follow-up notes, and connector settings
  • Reminder data needed to schedule local or push notifications
  • Device push tokens if the user enables notifications
  • Diagnostic logs and analytics only if explicitly enabled in production

How information is used

  • To authenticate the user and provide access to their workspace
  • To store and retrieve tasks, projects, continuity records, and other user data
  • To schedule reminders and deliver notifications if the user enables them
  • To improve reliability, security, and product quality

Health information note

Any health-related notes or uploaded content are handled as user-provided information for organization and educational support. Miss Cathy is not a medical provider and does not offer diagnosis certainty or prescribing authority.

Data sharing

Data is not sold. Data may be processed by infrastructure providers used to operate the product, such as hosting, database, storage, authentication, and notification providers.

User controls

  • Users may update or delete their workspace data through the product or support channels
  • Users may disable reminders and notifications
  • Users may request deletion of their account and associated data, subject to operational or legal retention requirements
